<p><p>The UHMWPE fabric market comprises three primary types: woven, knitted, and non-woven fabrics. Woven UHMWPE fabrics are created through interlacing yarns, offering high strength and stability, making them suitable for applications like ballistic protection. Knitted UHMWPE fabrics feature interconnected loops, providing flexibility and comfort, ideal for applications such as clothing and protective gear. Non-woven UHMWPE fabrics are made from bonded fibers, offering versatility and cost-effectiveness for uses in filtration and geotextiles, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
