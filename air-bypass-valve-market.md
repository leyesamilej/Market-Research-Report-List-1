<p><p>The Air Bypass Valve market includes two main types: Pressure Bypass Valves and Self-operated Bypass Valves. Pressure Bypass Valves regulate airflow based on predetermined pressure levels, ensuring optimal system performance and preventing overpressure conditions. In contrast, Self-operated Bypass Valves automatically adjust airflow without external controls, responding dynamically to system changes. Both types are essential in various applications like HVAC systems and gas turbines, enhancing efficiency and reliability in air management and distribution systems.</p></p>
