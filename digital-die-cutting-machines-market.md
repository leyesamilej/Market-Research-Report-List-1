<p><ul><li>Software Based Die Cut Machines</li><li>Cartridge Based Die Cut Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital die cutting machines are categorized into software-based and cartridge-based systems. Software-based die cut machines utilize computer programs for designing and cutting various materials, offering high customization and flexibility in creating intricate shapes. In contrast, cartridge-based die cut machines rely on interchangeable cartridges that contain physical dies for cutting specific designs, often providing simplicity and ease of use. Both types cater to different user preferences, serving hobbyists and professionals in crafting, packaging, and industrial applications.</p></p>
