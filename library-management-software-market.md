<p><p>Library Management Software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based systems allow libraries to access the software via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. They facilitate remote access and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premise solutions are installed locally on library servers, providing greater control over data security and customization, but requiring higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to specific organizational needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
