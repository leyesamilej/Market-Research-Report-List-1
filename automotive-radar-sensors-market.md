<p><p>The automotive radar sensors market comprises three key types: long-range, medium-range, and short-range radar sensors. Long-range radar sensors, typically operating at distances over 150 meters, are used for adaptive cruise control and collision avoidance. Medium-range sensors, covering 30 to 150 meters, aid in lane change assistance and obstacle detection. Short-range radar sensors operate within 0 to 30 meters, primarily utilized for parking assistance and blind-spot detection. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing vehicle safety and autonomous driving capabilities.</p></p>
