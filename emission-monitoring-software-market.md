<p><p>Emission Monitoring Software encompasses various types tailored to track and manage pollutant emissions effectively. Continuous Emission Monitoring Software (CEMS) provides real-time data collection and analysis of emissions from industrial processes, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. In contrast, Predictive Emission Monitoring Software (PEMS) utilizes advanced algorithms and historical data to forecast emissions based on operational parameters. Together, these software solutions enhance regulatory compliance, support environmental sustainability efforts, and optimize operational efficiencies for industries subject to emissions standards.</p></p>
