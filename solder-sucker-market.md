<p><ul><li>Manual Solder Sucker</li><li>Electric Solder Sucker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solder sucker market is divided into two primary types: manual solder suckers and electric solder suckers. Manual solder suckers are handheld devices that use a spring-loaded plunger mechanism to create suction, effectively removing molten solder from PCB connections. They are cost-effective and simple to use. In contrast, electric solder suckers are powered tools that provide stronger suction for quicker and more efficient solder removal. They are ideal for professional applications but are typically more expensive and require a power source.</p></p>
