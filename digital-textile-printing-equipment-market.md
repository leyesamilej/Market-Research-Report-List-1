<p><strong>The Digital Textile Printing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct to Fabric</li><li>Direct to Garment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital textile printing equipment market encompasses Direct to Fabric (DTF) and Direct to Garment (DTG) printing technologies. Direct to Fabric printing directly applies ink onto fabric rolls, making it suitable for large-scale production of textiles such as upholstery and fashion fabrics. In contrast, Direct to Garment printing targets finished garments, allowing for high-detail designs and customization on items like t-shirts and hoodies. Both methods leverage digital technology for efficiency, vibrant colors, and reduced waste in the textile industry.</p></p>
