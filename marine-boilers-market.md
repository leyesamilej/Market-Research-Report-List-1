<p><strong>The Marine Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fire Tube Boiler</li><li>Water Tube Boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine boilers market comprises two main types: fire tube boilers and water tube boilers. Fire tube boilers feature combustion gases passing through tubes surrounded by water, making them compact and suitable for smaller vessels. In contrast, water tube boilers circulate water within tubes heated by external flames, allowing for higher pressures and efficient heat transfer, ideal for larger ships. Both types cater to specific operational needs, impacting efficiency, size, and performance in marine applications.</p></p>
