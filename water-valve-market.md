<p><p>The water valve market comprises various types, including quarter-turn valves, multi-turn valves, and control valves. Quarter-turn valves provide quick shut-off with a 90-degree turn, ideal for on/off applications. Multi-turn valves require several rotations to open or close, making them suitable for precise flow regulation. Control valves, designed for managing fluid flow and pressure, allow for automatic adjustments and are essential in complex systems. Each type serves distinct purposes in water management and distribution, enhancing efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
