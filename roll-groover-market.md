<p><p>The roll groover market is categorized into two main types: hydraulic and manual. Hydraulic roll groovers utilize pressurized hydraulic systems for efficient and precise pipe grooving, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, manual roll groovers rely on mechanical force, offering a more cost-effective and portable solution for smaller tasks or less demanding environments. Both types serve essential roles in plumbing and HVAC industries, catering to varying operational needs and user preferences.</p></p>
