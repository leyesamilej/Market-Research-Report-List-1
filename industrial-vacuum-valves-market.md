<p><ul><li>Low Vacuum Valves</li><li>High Vacuum Valves</li><li>Ultra-high Vacuum Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial vacuum valves market is categorized into three main types based on pressure levels. Low vacuum valves operate at pressures from atmospheric to around 1 mbar and are used in applications like packaging and drying. High vacuum valves function between 1 mbar and 10^-7 mbar, suitable for processes like semiconductor manufacturing. Ultra-high vacuum valves operate below 10^-7 mbar, essential for research and specialized industries such as particle accelerators and thermonuclear fusion, where extremely low pressures are critical.</p></p>
