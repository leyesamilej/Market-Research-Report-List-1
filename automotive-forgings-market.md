<p><strong>The Automotive Forgings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forgings</li><li>Open Die Forgings</li><li>Rolled Rings Forgings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive forgings market consists of three main types: closed die forgings, open die forgings, and rolled rings forgings. Closed die forgings involve shaping heated metal within a mold, offering high precision and strength, making them ideal for parts like engine components. Open die forgings allow for more versatility in shape but require extensive machining, suitable for larger components. Rolled rings forgings create seamless rings used in applications like gears and bearings, providing enhanced durability and efficiency in automotive systems.</p></p>
