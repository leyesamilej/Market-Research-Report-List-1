<p><p>Chemical accelerators are compounds used to speed up chemical reactions, particularly in rubber manufacturing and other polymer industries. The market is primarily divided into primary and secondary accelerators. Primary accelerators initiate the curing process and determine the speed of vulcanization, while secondary accelerators enhance the effectiveness of primary ones and improve the overall properties of the finished product. Both types are essential in optimizing performance, durability, and efficiency in various applications, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
