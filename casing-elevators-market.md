<p><ul><li>Side Door</li><li>Counterpart</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Casing elevators are crucial in the oil and gas industry for transporting casing pipes during drilling operations. The market primarily consists of two types: side door casing elevators and counterpart casing elevators. Side door casing elevators offer easy access for loading and unloading pipes from the side, enhancing operational efficiency. Counterpart casing elevators, on the other hand, are designed to accommodate various pipe sizes and weights, providing versatility and stability during handling. Both types cater to the diverse needs of drilling operations.</p></p>
