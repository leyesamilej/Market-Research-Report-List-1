<p><p>The wellhead valve market consists of manual and automatic valve types, each serving distinct operational needs in oil and gas extraction. Manual valves require human intervention for opening and closing, offering simplicity and reliability but requiring more labor. In contrast, automatic valves utilize control systems to operate without direct human action, enhancing efficiency and safety by allowing remote adjustments. The choice between manual and automatic valves often depends on operational requirements, budget considerations, and desired levels of automation and control.</p></p>
