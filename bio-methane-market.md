<p><ul><li>Fermentation</li><li>Gasification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bio-methane market encompasses two primary types: fermentation and gasification. Fermentation involves anaerobic digestion of organic materials, such as agricultural waste, to produce biogas, which can be upgraded to bio-methane. Gasification, on the other hand, involves thermally converting biomass into syngas, which can be further processed into bio-methane. Both methods offer sustainable energy solutions, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and contribute to waste management by utilizing organic materials effectively in the production of renewable energy.</p></p>
