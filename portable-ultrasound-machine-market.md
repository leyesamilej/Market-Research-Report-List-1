<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Portable Ultrasound Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-mode</li><li>B-mode or 2D mode</li><li>C-mode</li><li>M-mode</li><li>Doppler mode</li><li>Pulse inversion mode</li><li>Harmonic mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable ultrasound machine market encompasses various imaging modes that cater to specific medical needs. A-mode provides a one-dimensional display for depth measurement, while B-mode (2D) creates grayscale images for detailed anatomical visualization. C-mode offers cross-sectional imaging, M-mode captures motion in real-time, and Doppler mode assesses blood flow. Pulse inversion enhances image clarity, and harmonic mode improves resolution by utilizing harmonic frequencies. Each mode contributes uniquely to diagnostics, expanding the utility of portable ultrasound devices in medical practices.</p></p>
