<p><p>Radiofrequency ablation (RFA) generators are classified into monopolar and multipolar types based on their operation mode. Monopolar generators utilize a single electrode to deliver energy, creating a thermal effect at the target tissue while grounding through a return electrode, ensuring efficient energy transfer. In contrast, multipolar generators employ multiple electrodes, allowing for simultaneous energy delivery to various tissue areas, enhancing precision and control during the procedure. Both types cater to distinct clinical needs, optimizing patient outcomes in various therapeutic applications.</p></p>
