<p><p>The WiFi smart light switch market features two primary types: in-wall switches and stick-on switches. In-wall switches are designed for fixed installation, replacing traditional switch panels and offering seamless integration into existing electrical systems. They enable control of lighting through smartphones or voice commands. Stick-on switches, on the other hand, provide flexibility as they can be attached to various surfaces without installation. These portable options are ideal for temporary setups, making them suitable for renters or those seeking easy installation.</p></p>
