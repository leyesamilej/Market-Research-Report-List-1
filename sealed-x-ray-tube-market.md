<p><p>The Sealed X-ray Tube market consists of two main types: Fixed Anode X-ray Tubes and Rotating Anode X-ray Tubes. Fixed Anode X-ray Tubes have a stationary anode, suitable for applications requiring lower power and less heat generation, making them ideal for portable or less demanding systems. In contrast, Rotating Anode X-ray Tubes feature a rotating anode, allowing for higher power output and better heat dissipation, making them suitable for advanced imaging systems in hospitals and specialized radiology applications.</p></p>
