Conversely, tank-type structures encapsulate the SF6 gas within a sealed tank, offering enhanced protection from environmental factors. Both designs are crucial in high-voltage applications for efficient interruption of electrical currents, ensuring safety and reliability in power distribution systems.</p></p>
