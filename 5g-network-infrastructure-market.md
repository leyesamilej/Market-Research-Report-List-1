<p><strong>The 5G Network Infrastructure Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Femto Cell</li><li>Pico Cell</li><li>Micro Cell</li><li>Macro Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G network infrastructure consists of various cell types categorized by their coverage and capacity. **Femto cells** are small, low-power nodes designed for home or small office use, enhancing indoor coverage. **Pico cells** offer slightly larger coverage, suitable for dense urban areas or buildings. **Micro cells** provide broader coverage than pico cells, ideal for urban environments and high-traffic locations. **Macro cells** are large towers covering extensive areas, essential for wide-ranging connectivity and backbone support in the 5G ecosystem.</p></p>
