Leak testers detect the integrity of sealed medical devices, crucial for maintaining sterility and function. Air quality analyzers monitor the environmental conditions for safe use of medical equipment. Other testers may include mechanical or functional testers that assess overall device performance, ensuring compliance and safety in medical settings.</p></p>
