<p><ul><li>Monocular Display</li><li>Binocular Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Helmet-Mounted Display (HMD) market for aircraft is primarily divided into two types: monocular and binocular displays. Monocular displays project information onto one eye, providing pilots with critical situational awareness while maintaining visibility of the environment. In contrast, binocular displays offer a stereo view, displaying data to both eyes, enhancing depth perception and immersive experience. These technologies are essential for modern aviation, improving pilot performance by integrating flight data and tactical information into a cohesive visual interface.</p></p>
