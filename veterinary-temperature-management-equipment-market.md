<p><p>The veterinary temperature management equipment market encompasses various technologies to maintain optimal body temperature in animals during medical procedures. Fluid warmers heat intravenous fluids to prevent hypothermia, ensuring a stable temperature during surgery. Convection warming systems circulate warm air around the animal, providing a gentle heat source that promotes comfort. Conduction warming systems involve heated surfaces or blankets that directly transfer warmth to the animal, effectively stabilizing their temperature and enhancing recovery outcomes in veterinary care.</p></p>
