<p><p>The ethanol gas detection equipment market includes several sensor types. Molecular Property Spectrometer Sensors analyze molecular vibrations to detect ethanol concentrations accurately. Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Sensors measure the absorption of infrared light by ethanol molecules, providing reliable detection. Catalytic Gas Sensors rely on chemical reactions to identify the presence of ethanol, often used in industrial applications. Other sensor types may include semiconductor sensors or electrochemical detectors, offering diverse options for effective ethanol monitoring across various environments.</p></p>
