<p><p>The tapioca starch market is divided into two main types: original starch and modified starch. Original starch is extracted directly from cassava tubers and is primarily used in food applications, providing thickening and binding properties. Modified starch, on the other hand, undergoes physical or chemical processes to enhance its functional properties, making it suitable for a broader range of applications, such as in food, textiles, and pharmaceuticals. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, reflecting their unique functionalities.</p></p>
