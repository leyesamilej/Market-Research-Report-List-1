<p><p>Natural Hydraulic Lime (NHL) is a sustainable building material derived from limestone that undergoes hydraulic setting upon contact with water. The NHL market is categorized into three main types based on their strength and performance: NHL2, NHL3.5, and NHL5. NHL2 is the mildest, suitable for interior applications, while NHL3.5 offers moderate strength for general use in masonry and plasters. NHL5, the strongest, is ideal for heavy-duty construction, such as structural repairs and exposed external applications. Each type caters to specific construction needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
