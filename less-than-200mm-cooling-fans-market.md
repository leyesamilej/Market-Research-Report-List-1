<p><p>The less than 200mm cooling fans market primarily includes three types: centrifugal fans, axial fans, and others. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to increase air pressure and are effective for high static pressures, ideal for applications with restricted airflow. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan axis, offering high airflow rates and energy efficiency for general cooling. The "others" category encompasses specialized designs, such as bladeless fans or miniature models tailored for specific industrial or consumer requirements.</p></p>
