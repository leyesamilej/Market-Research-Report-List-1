<p><strong>The Vacuum Cables for Semiconductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Vacuum (HV)</li><li>Ultra-high Vacuum (UHV)</li><li>Extreme High Vacuum (XHV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum cables for the semiconductor market are categorized based on the level of vacuum they maintain. High Vacuum (HV) systems operate between 10^-3 to 10^-7 torr, suitable for general semiconductor processes. Ultra-high Vacuum (UHV) systems, ranging from 10^-7 to 10^-9 torr, are essential for advanced applications like thin film deposition. Extreme High Vacuum (XHV), achieving pressures below 10^-9 torr, is used in cutting-edge research, ensuring minimal contamination and precise control for sensitive semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
