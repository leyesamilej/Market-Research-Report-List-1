<p><p>The MRI-guided neurosurgical ablation market encompasses various technologies aimed at precisely targeting and treating neurological conditions. MRI-guided laser therapy systems use focused laser energy to vaporize tissue, minimizing damage to surrounding structures. MRI-guided RF ablation systems utilize radiofrequency energy to heat and destroy abnormal tissue. MRI-guided focused ultrasound systems employ ultrasonic waves to thermally ablate tissue non-invasively. Other technologies in this market may include hybrid systems and innovative techniques that leverage MRI for enhanced visualization and treatment accuracy.</p></p>
