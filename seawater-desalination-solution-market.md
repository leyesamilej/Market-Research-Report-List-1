<p><p>The seawater desalination solutions market is mainly categorized into three types: Reverse Osmosis (RO), Multi-Effect Distillation (MED), and others. RO utilizes semi-permeable membranes to separate salt and impurities from water, making it efficient and energy-saving. MED employs multiple stages of evaporation and condensation to convert seawater into fresh water, often requiring more energy. Other methods include thermal distillation and electrodialysis, which cater to diverse applications and technological preferences in addressing water scarcity challenges globally.</p></p>
