<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Balancing Weight Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clip-On Type</li><li>Adhesive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel balancing weight market comprises two primary types: clip-on and adhesive weights. Clip-on weights are typically utilized with steel wheels and attach directly to the rim's edge, easily allowing for adjustments. In contrast, adhesive weights are designed for alloy wheels, adhering to the wheel's inner surface to maintain aesthetics and aerodynamic performance. Both types serve the crucial purpose of ensuring smooth vehicle operation by balancing the wheels, enhancing tire life, and improving safety and ride quality.</p></p>
