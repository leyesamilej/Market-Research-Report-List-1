<p><p>The Sensor and Detector Interface Market is divided into two main types: Analog and Digital. In contrast, Digital interfaces convert signals into binary code, facilitating easier integration with digital systems and offering enhanced noise immunity, accuracy, and the ability to process complex data. Each type serves distinct functions, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
