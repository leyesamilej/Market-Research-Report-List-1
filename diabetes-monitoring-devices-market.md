<p><p>The diabetes monitoring devices market comprises two primary types: non-invasive and invasive monitoring devices. Non-invasive devices include technologies that measure glucose levels without penetrating the skin, such as continuous glucose monitors (CGMs) and smartwatches. Invasive monitoring devices involve techniques that require skin penetration, like fingerstick blood glucose meters and implantable sensors. Both types play crucial roles in diabetes management, offering patients varying degrees of convenience, comfort, and accuracy in tracking blood glucose levels for better health outcomes.</p></p>
