<p><p>LPWAN asset trackers utilize low-power wide-area network technologies to efficiently monitor and locate assets over long distances. Cellular LPWAN, such as NB-IoT and LTE-M, leverages existing cellular infrastructure for reliable connectivity, ideal for urban environments. Conversely, LoRa (Long Range) operates on unlicensed frequency bands, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness in rural areas, with longer range capabilities. Both market types enable real-time tracking, improving supply chain efficiency and asset management while minimizing power consumption.</p></p>
