<p><strong>The Table Top Autoclave Sterilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class B</li><li>Class N</li><li>Class S</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tabletop autoclave sterilizers are categorized into Class B, Class N, and Class S based on their features and sterilization capabilities. Class B sterilizers are versatile, providing high-temperature steam sterilization suitable for a variety of instruments, including porous loads. Class N sterilizers are designed primarily for sterilizing non-porous instruments and do not have a vacuum feature. Class S sterilizers are a hybrid, suitable for some porous items but limited compared to Class B, making them ideal for dental and small clinic use.</p></p>
