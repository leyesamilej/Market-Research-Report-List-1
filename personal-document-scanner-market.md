<p><p>The personal document scanner market primarily features three types of imaging technologies: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), CIS (Contact Image Sensor), and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD scanners deliver high-quality images and are often used in professional settings for their superior light sensitivity. CIS scanners are compact and energy-efficient, making them ideal for portable devices. CMOS technology, known for its low power consumption and faster processing speeds, is gaining traction in consumer markets, providing versatile solutions for personal document scanning needs.</p></p>
