<p><p>Calcium sulfate is a naturally occurring inorganic compound with the chemical formula CaSO₄. It exists in various forms, including anhydrite (anhydrous calcium sulfate) and gypsum (hydrated calcium sulfate). It is widely used in industries such as construction, agriculture, food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. In construction, it is a key component in plaster, drywall, and cement. In agriculture, it serves as a soil conditioner, while in food, it acts as a coagulant and firming agent. Its pharmaceutical applications include use as an excipient in tablets and as a desiccant.</p><p>The Calcium Sulfate Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.3% during the forecast period.
