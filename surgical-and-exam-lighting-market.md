<p><p>The surgical and exam lighting market comprises two main types: LED and halogen. LED lights are gaining popularity due to their energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and lower heat emission, making them ideal for prolonged procedures. They provide bright, adjustable illumination with a high color rendering index, ensuring accurate color perception during surgeries. Halogen lights, while traditional, offer bright, consistent lighting and are less expensive upfront. However, they generate more heat and have a shorter lifespan compared to LEDs, impacting long-term operational costs.</p></p>
