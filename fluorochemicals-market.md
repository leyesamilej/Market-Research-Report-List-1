<p><p>The fluorochemicals market includes various types such as PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), known for its non-stick and heat-resistant properties, widely used in cookware and industrial applications. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) offers chemical resistance and is used in piping, coatings, and lithium-ion batteries. FKM (Fluorocarbon Elastomers) are heat and chemical-resistant elastomers used in seals and gaskets. FSR (Fluorosilicone Rubber) combines silicone and fluorocarbon properties for extreme environments. FFKM (Perfluoroelastomers) provide superior chemical resistance for harsh conditions. Other fluorochemicals include specialized materials for diverse industrial, automotive, and electronics applications.</p></p>
