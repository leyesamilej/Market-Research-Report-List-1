<p><ul><li>Rotary Sorter</li><li>Cross Belt Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cap sorter market types include rotary sorters and cross belt sorters. Rotary sorters utilize a circular motion to direct items to designated lanes, optimizing space and efficiency in high-speed environments. They are ideal for lightweight products and high-throughput applications. In contrast, cross belt sorters transport items on individual belts moving perpendicular to the main flow, allowing for precise sorting of various sizes and weights. Both systems enhance operational efficiency and accuracy in material handling and distribution processes.</p></p>
