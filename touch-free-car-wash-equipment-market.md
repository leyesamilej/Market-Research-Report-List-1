<p><p>The touch-free car wash equipment market includes two primary types: gantry car wash systems and conveyor tunnel car wash systems. Gantry car washes utilize a stationary overhead framework that moves down over the vehicle, delivering high-pressure water and cleaning agents without physical contact. In contrast, conveyor tunnel systems transport vehicles through a continuous cleaning line, using various brushes and sprays for an efficient wash. Both systems emphasize convenience and thorough cleaning while minimizing the risk of vehicle damage.</p></p>
