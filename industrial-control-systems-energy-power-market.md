<p><p>Industrial Control Systems (ICS) in the energy and power sector encompass various technologies to manage and monitor industrial processes. SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) systems enable centralized control of remote operations. PLCs (Programmable Logic Controllers) are used for automation of machinery on the factory floor. DCS (Distributed Control Systems) manage complex processes across multiple locations. HMIs (Human-Machine Interfaces) facilitate interaction between operators and machines. Additional market types include safety systems, commercial software, and advanced analytics solutions, enhancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
