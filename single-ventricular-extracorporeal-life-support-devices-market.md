<p><ul><li>VA</li><li>VV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-ventricular extracorporeal life support (ECLS) device market primarily consists of two types: Veno-Arterial (VA) and Veno-Venous (VV) support systems. VA devices assist patients with both cardiac and respiratory failure by providing oxygenation and circulatory support, diverting blood from veins and returning it to the arteries. In contrast, VV devices focus solely on respiratory support, facilitating oxygenation and carbon dioxide removal without impacting blood circulation. Each device type addresses specific patient needs, shaping distinct market segments.</p></p>
