<p><p>Sample Rate Converter ICs are essential in digital signal processing, enabling the adaptation of different sampling rates between systems. The market for these ICs is divided into two primary types: asynchronous and synchronous. Asynchronous sample rate converters allow for conversion between non-matching sample rates without requiring a common clock, making them versatile for various applications. In contrast, synchronous converters operate with a shared clock, providing precise timing but typically limited to systems with matching clock rates. Both types serve critical roles in audio processing, telecommunications, and multimedia applications.</p></p>
