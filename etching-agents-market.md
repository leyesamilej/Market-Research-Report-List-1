<p><p>The etching agents market is primarily divided into two categories: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching agents involve liquid chemicals that dissolve material from a substrate, commonly used in semiconductor manufacturing for precise patterning. In contrast, dry etching agents utilize gases and plasma to achieve etching through chemical reactions or physical bombardment, offering greater control and resolution. Both types are crucial for industries like electronics and materials science, driving advancements in manufacturing processes and technology.</p></p>
