<p><ul><li>Biochemical</li><li>Thermochemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biorefinery plant market can be categorized into two main types: biochemical and thermochemical. Biochemical processes use enzymatic or microbial methods to convert organic materials into biofuels, chemicals, and other valuable products, leveraging biological reactions. In contrast, thermochemical processes involve the application of heat to convert biomass into biofuels, such as syngas or biochar, typically through methods like gasification or pyrolysis. Both approaches aim to enhance sustainable energy production while minimizing waste and optimizing resource utilization.</p></p>
