<p><ul><li>Single Cup</li><li>Double Cup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Couplant Heater market is divided into two primary types: Single Cup and Double Cup heaters. Single Cup heaters are designed for individual users, providing localized heating for specific applications, making them ideal for small-scale nondestructive testing (NDT) scenarios. In contrast, Double Cup heaters feature two heating compartments, allowing simultaneous heating of multiple couplant materials, which enhances efficiency in larger testing environments. Both types play essential roles in ensuring optimal performance during ultrasonic testing by maintaining the necessary temperature for effective coupling.</p></p>
