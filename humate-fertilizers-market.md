<p><p>Humate fertilizers are categorized into two main market types: solid and liquid. Solid humate fertilizers are typically granules or powders, offering slow-release nutrients and easy application for soil enhancement. They improve soil structure and microbial activity. In contrast, liquid humate fertilizers are concentrated solutions that provide quick nutrient uptake, ideal for foliar applications or irrigation systems. Both types enhance plant growth, but their application methods and nutrient release rates differ, catering to various agricultural needs and preferences.</p></p>
