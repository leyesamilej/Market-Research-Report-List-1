<p><p>The high-temperature heat pump market offers several types to meet diverse heating needs. Air to water monobloc heat pumps are compact, integrating components into a single unit for easier installation. Air to water cylinder heat pumps utilize a separate cylinder for heated water storage. Groundwater heat pumps extract heat from underground sources, providing efficient heating. Split air-to-water heat pumps consist of outdoor and indoor units, enhancing flexibility. Exhaust air heat pumps recover heat from used indoor air, maximizing energy efficiency in residential settings.</p></p>
