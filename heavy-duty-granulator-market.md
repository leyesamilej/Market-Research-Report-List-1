<p><p>The Heavy Duty Granulator market is segmented into dry and wet types, each serving distinct processing needs. Dry granulators utilize mechanical force to compact and size materials without the use of liquids, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal moisture content. In contrast, wet granulators incorporate liquids to facilitate granulation, enhancing the consistency and uniformity of the final product. Both types play critical roles in industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and plastics, tailoring granulation methods to specific material properties.</p></p>
