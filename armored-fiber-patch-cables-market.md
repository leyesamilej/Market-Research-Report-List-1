<p><ul><li>Single-mode Fiber Patch Cable</li><li>Multi-mode Fiber Patch Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The armored fiber patch cables market consists of two primary types: single-mode and multi-mode fiber patch cables. Single-mode fiber patch cables, designed for long-distance communication, utilize a single light path, allowing for high bandwidth and minimal signal loss over extensive distances. Conversely, multi-mode fiber patch cables are suitable for shorter distances, featuring multiple light paths that accommodate multiple signals simultaneously. Both types offer enhanced durability and protection against physical damage, making them ideal for demanding environments in telecommunications and data centers.</p></p>
